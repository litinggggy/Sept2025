Programmes Offered

The School of Computing offers a range of programmes designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in the field of computer science.

  • Diploma in Computer Science – A programme focusing on programming, networking, and system fundamentals.
  • Certificate in Information Technology – An introductory course offering basic IT and computing skills.
  • Short Courses & Workshops – Training sessions on web development, cybersecurity, and software tools.

Each programme is structured to prepare students for real-world careers and further studies.

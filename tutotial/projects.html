Student Projects Showcase

The School of Computing encourages students to apply their skills through hands-on projects. Below are examples of work created by our students.

  • Web Development Projects: Students design responsive websites using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • Mobile Applications: Simple apps that demonstrate creativity and problem-solving.
  • Database Projects: Projects involving data storage, retrieval and real-world use cases.

These projects help students build confidence and gain practical experience in computing.
